‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Why do both people get Gems?

Because boring referral programs only reward the inviter! We believe new users deserve a welcome bonus too. Happy users = better conversations for everyone.

Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?

Nope! Refer as many friends as you want. The more the merrier (and the more Gems you earn!).

When do I get my Gems?

Gems are credited instantly when your referral completes their first 2-minute video conversation. You'll see them in your balance right away!

Can I cash out my referral Gems?

Yes! Referral gems are spendable immediately (tips, purchases, etc.). They become cashable once your referral makes their first purchase, OR stays active for 30+ days with 5+ conversations. This vesting system protects against fraud and ensures you're rewarded for bringing real, engaged users. Tips you receive from hosting are always immediately cashable. Minimum cashout is 10,000 Gems ($50). See our Cashout page for details.

What counts as "completing" a conversation?

The referred user must join a video room and stay for at least 2 minutes of active conversation. Spectating doesn't count - they need to participate!
