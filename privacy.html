1. Introduction

ChatSpheres ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our video chat platform.

This policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU users and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide Services: Enable video calls, spectating, recordings, and social features
  • Process Payments: Handle subscriptions, Gem purchases, and creator payouts
  • Improve Platform: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
  • Communicate: Send service updates, marketing (with consent), and support responses
  • Ensure Safety: Detect fraud, enforce policies, protect users
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with laws, respond to legal requests

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

4.1 We Share Data With:

  • Service Providers: Stripe (payments), Supabase (database), LiveKit (video), Netlify (hosting)
  • Other Users: Your public profile, video presence when in rooms
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect rights/safety

4.2 We Never:

  • Sell your personal data to advertisers
  • Share private video content without consent
  • Use your likeness for advertising without permission

7. Your Rights

7.1 All Users Can:

  • Access: View your personal data in account settings
  • Correct: Update inaccurate information
  • Delete: Remove your account and associated data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails

7.2 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

EU residents have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Erasure: Request complete deletion of your data within 30 days
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Complain: Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

7.3 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-out of data sales (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)

While we take extensive precautions, no system is 100% secure. Please protect your account credentials.
